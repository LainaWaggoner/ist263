Interviews with Syracuse Students on Fashion and Individuality

Interview with Avery Rush (Student Minoring in Fashion Design, Editor of UGirl Magazine)

Q: What is most essential in creating the perfect outfit?
A: “Start with a good base like a white tank top or a pair of good denim jeans and style it from there. Don’t forget about shoes; a great pair can totally elevate the look! Oh, and a little confidence goes a long way —when you feel good in what you’re wearing, it shows.”
Q: What are you wearing right now?
A: “I’m wearing a Brandy Melville T-shirt I got off Depop a few years ago and Abercrombie Low-Waist medium-wash denim jeans that I got only a week or so ago. My shoes are sambas, and my accessories are all over the place but I’ve been having a lot of fun mix-matching gold/silver jewelry. Oh! And this necklace is from the brand Distorted Jewelry, they have the best statement pieces.”
Q: What’s your favorite trend you’ve seen on Syracuse Campus recently?
A: “I’ve been seeing a lot of Jorts since the weather is still warm and I love it! Also, any cool tote bags will catch my eye.”
