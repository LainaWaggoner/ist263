Interviews with Syracuse Students on Fashion and Individuality

Interview with Avery Rush (Student Minoring in Fashion Design, Editor of UGirl Magazine)

Q: What is most essential in creating the perfect outfit?
A: Start with a good base like a white tank top or a pair of good denim jeans and style it from there.
Q: What are you wearing right now?
A: I’m wearing a Brandy Melville T-shirt, Abercrombie jeans, sambas, and a mix of gold/silver jewelry.
Q: What’s your favorite trend you’ve seen on Syracuse Campus recently?
A: Jorts and cool tote bags are definitely my favorite trends right now.
